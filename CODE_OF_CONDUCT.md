# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want Moon Mustache to be a welcoming, respectful, and technically constructive project.

Participants, maintainers, and contributors should help make discussion safe for people with different backgrounds, experience levels, and perspectives.

## Expected Behavior

- be respectful and professional
- assume good intent while still being honest about problems
- give actionable technical feedback
- welcome questions from newer contributors
- focus on improving the project, not winning arguments

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment, threats, or intimidation
- discriminatory language or personal attacks
- deliberate disruption of technical collaboration
- publishing private information without permission
- hostile or repeated bad-faith behavior after feedback

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or limit participation when behavior harms the project community.

Serious or repeated violations may lead to removal from project spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer privately when possible and include enough detail for review.

We will try to handle reports fairly, with discretion, and with the goal of restoring a healthy collaboration environment.
